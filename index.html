Why this Portal?

To act as a catalyst in streamlining the procurement processes of public/ Government sector with the help of ICT tools and technologies, enabling them to harness the multidimensional benefits of eProcurement/ eTendering pertaining to: efficient and costeffective procurement, shortened procurement cycles, full transparency in the whole

Learn More

Tender Offer

An offer to purchase some or all of shareholders' shares in a corporation. Offers are typically made publicly and invite shareholders to sell their shares for a specified price and within a particular window of time.

Takeover Bid

A corporate action in which an acquiring company presents an offer to a target company in attempt to assume control of it

Request For Proposal (RFP)

A project funding announcement posted by a business or organization for which companies can place bids to complete the project.e

Major goals and guiding objectives

Point 1

The prime objective is to educate, encourage/ incentivize and thus facilitate Government Departments at all levels, through appropriate steps and interventions from Central Government level, to readily adopt and use the Central Public Procurement Portal to bring in transparency in tendering process and gradually move towards adoption of electronic Procurement solution(s) for their procurement needs on a continuing basis.

Point 2

Process, avoidance of human discretion/ interference to the extent possible, easy availability of complete audit trail and evidential data etc.

Point 3

Enable access to widest reach of tenders and unhindered secured bid submission facility for all, from any corner of the country.
